# Concrete-Strength-Prediction

This project aims to investigate and analyze the compressive strength of concrete, a critical property that directly influences the structural integrity and durability of concrete structures. The research involves a systematic study of various factors affecting compressive strength, including mix design, curing conditions, aggregate properties, and water-cement ratio. Experimental testing will be conducted using standardized procedures to measure and evaluate the compressive strength of concrete specimens at different ages. The results will be statistically analyzed to discern patterns and correlations, providing valuable insights into optimizing concrete mixtures for enhanced strength and performance. This research contributes to the advancement of concrete technology and informs best practices in construction and infrastructure development.
